Meet Dex.

Take Codex with you anywhere, on any Android device.

Get it on Google Play View Source

Everything you need, in your pocket

End-to-End Encrypted

Secure pairing between your Android device and Mac. Message contents are encrypted after the session handshake — the relay never sees them.

Git From Your Phone

Commit, push, pull, and switch branches from anywhere. Review diffs and manage your repos without touching your laptop.

Live Streaming

Watch Codex work in real time on your phone while it runs on your Mac. Steer active runs and queue follow-up prompts mid-turn.

Shared Thread History

Your conversation history stays in sync between Dex and Codex on your Mac. Pick up where you left off on either device.

Skills & Slash Commands

Run slash commands, trigger skills, and use subagents right from your phone. Full Codex control without the keyboard.

Local-First & Open Source

No cloud accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Everything runs on your devices. The full source is on GitHub — inspect, fork, and self-host.

Open source, self-host friendly

Dex is part of the Remodex project. Run your own relay, inspect every line of code, and keep full control of your data.
